This is a visualiser of 2-generated subgroups of $\text{SL}(2, \mathbb{C})$ in hyperbolic 3-space.

$\text{PSL}(2, \mathbb{C})$ (the group of $2\times 2$ invertible complex matrices, up to scaling) has a natural action on $\mathbb{H}^3$ (3-dimensional hyperbolic space) by isometries. Given a finite set $S$ of matrices, we may visualise the group $G$ generated by $S$ by the embedding of its Cayley graph in hyperbolic space. That is, we construct a graph whose vertex set is the orbit of some vertex $v$ (the "origin"), and whose edge set is pairs $\{gv, gsv\}$, where $g \in G$ and $s \in S$. This app visualises these graphs in $\mathbb{H}^3$ using the Poincaré ball model.
